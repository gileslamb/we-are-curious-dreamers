Distance to the Moon — Original Soundtrack Out Now

Inspired by Italo Calvino's Cosmicomics, Distance to the Moon began as a surreal orchestral suite. Wide, dreamlike, slightly strange. A musical response to the idea of reaching for something vast and impossible.

Over time, the project evolved into a beautiful and profound stop motion animation film. The film reshaped the score. What started as something expansive became more intimate and personal. The final award-winning soundtrack centres on a fragile felt piano theme, subtle textures, and a sense of memory suspended in space.

The original soundtrack album is now available on all major streaming platforms.

We have also created an immersive website where you can experience the music in a deeper, more reflective way.

If you have followed the journey, thank you. If you are discovering it now, we hope you step inside.
