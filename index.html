Who We Are

We Are Curious Dreamers is a creative studio and event space for curious minds, where music, film, digital technology, and imagination come together.

Founded by BAFTA-nominated producer/director Sacha Kyle and award-winning composer Giles Lamb, we curate experiences that spark dialogue, nourish the human spirit, and explore how creativity can heal, connect, and inspire.

Our events are free to attend — because we believe that culture and conversation should be open to everyone. Contributions from our community help us grow the programme, improve the space, and develop new creative projects.

◉
Discovery
Talks, screenings, and demonstrations that open new worlds of thought and possibility.
◎
Connection
Fireside chats and discussions designed to bring people together in shared experience.
◈
Creation
Workshops and collaborative sessions that invite you to make, experiment, and play.
◇
Innovation
A living laboratory where music, film, neuroscience, and technology converge.
